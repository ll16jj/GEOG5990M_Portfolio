About Me

Hi! My name is Jordan Johnson and I am a student at the Univeristy of Leeds, welcome to my portfolio homepage! I have been at the Univeristy of Leeds for a few years, I initially studied an undergraduate degree in Geological Sciences however, I am currently studying a masters in GIS. I became gravitated to GIS and data analysis through constant use in my Geological Sciences degree but, also a consequence of my year in industry at Getech Group Plc. During my time at Getech, I worked on Multi-Sat 2020, a global database of gravity data derived from satellite data. Throughout this project I was exposed to various software and scripting languages such as, Python, ArcPro, Linux, Matlab, Geosoft and in-built softwares such as, GetGrid and Valsatt. This portfolio page is an accumulation of some of the projects I have produced during my time studying my masters, just click on Projects on the navigation bar to learn more!
